About Technofi

Technofi is an annual technical festival organized by IIT Roorkee. It is one of the largest technical festivals in Asia. The festival is a platform for students to showcase their technical prowess. With a variety of competitions and quizzes, the festival is a haven for tech enthusiasts. The festival also hosts a number of workshops and seminars that provide students with the opportunity to learn from experts in the field. Technofi aims to inspire and engage the next generation of technologists.
